Austrian Championships

Vienna Roller Derby, Fearless Bruisers (Innsbruck), Steel City Rollers (Linz) supported by SBG Knockouts (Salzburg) and Dust City Rollers (Graz) are competing for the title of Austrian champion in 6 games over 2 days. There will also be an exciting entertainment program between games!

21 August 2021

Newbie day and Open practice

We are looking for new skaters again, this is your chance to start playing roller derby! If you want to become part of our team, sign up for our Newbie Day on 25 September 1-5 p.m. at Sportunion Gaußgasse 3, 8010 Graz. We will provide all the equipment you need, teach you the basics and tell you everything about our team and what it means to be a Dusty. ...
